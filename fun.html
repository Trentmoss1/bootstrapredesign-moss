More Fun!

If you are looking for indoor entertainment, there are plenty of options the whole family can enjoy. Museams, thaters, concerts, and during some parts of the year even the circus! Go downtown and check out the quilt museam before walking to Court Square Theater and watching a movie while enjoying freshly made popcorn. Need to do some shopping? Go to Valley Mall on east market street and walk around. Plenty of stores, places to eat, and even a place to have a family Nerf war! Then, on your way out, you cannot help but notice the giant circus tent that comes to town only certain times of the year. Something you definitly don't want to miss.
