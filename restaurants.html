Restaurants

Downtown Harrisonburg is packed full of restaurants ranging from fine dining to dive bars all within walking distance of eachother. With an almost overwhelming selection of cuisines, there is something for everybody. Whether you want a burger and fries or a nice steak dinner, you will find the right place just by walking around the area. With there only being three meals in a day, it would take you more than a week to try all of them!
