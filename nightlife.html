Night Life

After a long day of exploring all that the friendly city has to offeer, its time to take a load off. Come downtown to the most popular bars in Harrisonburg and get some drinks! Play games like pool, pingpong, and even bowling at Ruby's arcade. Late Dinner? Go to Billy Jacks or Jack Browns for some burgers, beer, and sports. In the mood to dance? Check out Ruby's lounge or Dukes bar for loud music and lots of energy. When visiting Harrisonburg make sure to save some energy during the day so you won't miss out on the fun when the sun goes down.
